# Niches

**ID:** quant-niches
**Branch:** Quantitative Finance

## Description

Quantitative Finance institution types and market niches including:

- **HFT Firms** - High-frequency trading firms using ultra-low latency systems
- **Systemic Hedge Funds** - Quantitative hedge funds managing systematic strategies
- **Prop Trading** - Proprietary trading firms deploying firm capital
- **Market Makers** - Firms providing liquidity through continuous quoting
- **Investment Bank Strats** - Quantitative strategists at major investment banks
