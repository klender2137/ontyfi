# Niches

**ID:** asset-niches
**Branch:** Global Asset Management Institution

## Description

Asset Management institution types and market niches including:

- **Institutional Asset Managers** - Traditional asset managers offering mutual funds, ETFs, and institutional mandates
- **Hedge Funds** - Private investment partnerships pursuing absolute returns using advanced strategies
- **Sovereign Wealth Funds** - State-owned investment funds managing national reserves
- **Pension Funds** - Institutions managing retirement assets with liability matching
- **Family Offices** - Managing wealth for ultra-high-net-worth families
- **Insurance Company General Accounts** - Investment arms managing insurance premiums
- **Endowments & Foundations** - Long-term capital pools supporting institutions and charities
