# Niches

**ID:** fpa-niches
**Branch:** Financial Planning & Analysis

## Description

FP&A institution types and market niches including:

- **Corporate HQ FP&A** - Centralized teams responsible for consolidated budgets
- **Business Unit Finance** - Embedded teams working inside departments
- **Regional Finance** - Teams managing region-specific financial planning
- **FP&A Consulting** - External consultants providing financial modeling support
- **Private Equity Portfolio FP&A** - High-performance teams inside PE-owned companies
