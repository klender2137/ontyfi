# Niches

**ID:** pubfin-niches
**Branch:** Public Finance Sector

## Description

Public Finance institution types and market niches including:

- **Ministry of Finance / Treasury** - Central authority responsible for fiscal policy, national budgeting, and sovereign debt issuance
- **Central Bank** - Monetary authority responsible for price stability and financial system supervision
- **International Monetary Fund (IMF)** - Global institution providing financial support during sovereign debt crises
- **Tax & Customs Administration** - Government agency responsible for collecting taxes and enforcing customs laws
