# Niches

**ID:** vc-niches
**Branch:** Venture Capital

## Description

Venture Capital institution types and market niches including:

- **Institutional VC** - Traditional venture capital firms investing LP capital
- **Corporate VC** - Strategic investment arms of large corporations
- **Micro-VC** - Smaller funds focusing on seed and pre-seed investments
- **Venture Debt** - Lenders providing debt financing to venture-backed companies
- **Accelerators** - Programs providing capital, mentorship, and resources to early-stage startups
