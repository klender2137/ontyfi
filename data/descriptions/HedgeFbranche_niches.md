# Niches

**ID:** hedge-niches
**Branch:** Hedge Funds

## Description

Hedge Fund institution types and market niches including:

- **Global Macro Funds** - Trading based on macroeconomic trends and geopolitical events
- **Long-Short Equity Funds** - Taking both long and short positions in equity markets
- **Quantitative Funds** - Systematic trading using mathematical models and algorithms
- **Event-Driven Funds** - Capitalizing on corporate events like mergers and restructurings
- **Multi-Strategy Platforms** - Combining multiple hedge fund strategies under one roof
