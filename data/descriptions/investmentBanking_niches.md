# Niches

**ID:** ib-niches
**Branch:** M&A / Investment Banking

## Description

Investment Banking institution types and market niches including:

- **Bulge Bracket Banks** - Large global banks offering full-service investment banking
- **Elite Boutiques** - Specialized firms focused purely on M&A advisory
- **Middle Market Banks** - Regional firms serving mid-sized companies
- **Industry Boutiques** - Specialized firms focused on specific sectors
- **Regional Full-Service Banks** - Local banks combining commercial and investment banking
