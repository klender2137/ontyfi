# Niches

**ID:** risk-niches
**Branch:** Risk Management

## Description

Risk Management institution types and market niches including:

- **G-SIBs** - Global Systemically Important Banks with comprehensive risk frameworks
- **Insurance & Reinsurance** - Firms managing underwriting and catastrophic risk
- **Regulatory Bodies** - Government agencies supervising financial system stability
- **Credit Rating Agencies** - Organizations assessing creditworthiness of issuers
- **Risk Consulting** - Advisory firms providing risk management solutions
