# Niches

**ID:** pe-niches
**Branch:** Private Equity

## Description

Private Equity institution types and market niches including:

- **Mega Buyout** - Large-cap buyout funds targeting billion-dollar acquisitions
- **Middle Market PE** - Funds focused on mid-sized company investments
- **Growth Capital** - Funds providing expansion capital without full control
- **Co-Investment Vehicles** - Platforms for LPs to invest alongside main funds
- **Venture Debt** - Lending solutions for venture-backed startups
